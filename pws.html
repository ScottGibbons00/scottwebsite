Waterfall

Location:

This is a waterfall near Whittier, Alaska on the Prince William Sound.

Background:

The Prince William Sound is home to hundreds of glaciers, waterfalls, beaches, and other picturesque features. This one waterfall was home to hundreds of nesting birds, which can be seen in the side of the cliff

Why it's special:

To me, this picture seemed like it was from a puzzle or painting. So to see something like this in person was breath taking.

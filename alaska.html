Reflection

Location:

This picture is taken on a small lake nearing Dillingham Alaska.

Background:

This spot is very special because it is remote and quiet. The only unnatural sounds are the occaisional planes. Surprisingly, this picture was taken at around 1am, showing how much daylight there is in Alaska during the summer

Why it's special:

I took this picture because I thought the reflection was incredible. The water looked like a mirror, reflecting the light coming through the clouds.

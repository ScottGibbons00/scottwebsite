Dark Trees

Location:

This picture is taken in Killarney National Park in Ireland

Background:

My family recently traveled to Ireland, and we were so amazed by the gorgeous scenery. This national park is located towards the south of the country.

Why it's special:

One very notable part of Ireland was the mosses growing in the forests. It created the eire look that you can see in the picture. Entire forests were carpeted with moss, which was something I had never seen before.

Early Fall

Location:

This picture was taken on the Vermont side of the Connecticut near Hanover.

Background:

I took this picture also on a run near campus. I remember feeling surprised how flat the river looked and loved running along it in the fall.

Why it's special:

I took this picture becuase I loved the contrast between the yellow and green trees. This was in the early fall, and the leaves had just started to change colors. This created this really cool effect, which was enhanced by the reflection in the water

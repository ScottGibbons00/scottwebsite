Dusk on the Connecticut

Location:

This picture is taken close to Dartmouth's Organic Farm overlooking the Connecticut River

Background:

This picture was taken during fall term right as the sun was setting. I loved how the light was coming through the clouds accross the river

Why it's special:

One thing that I have really enjoed doing since coming to Dartmouth is exploring the area on my runs. Sometimes, I would get lost and it would start snowing. Other times, the timing would be just right for me to take a picture like this.
